Tutorial Sections

Please make sure you are enrolled in a tutorial on ACORN that fits with your timetable.

Tutorials will begin on Week 2 and will be ran by one of the course's TAs. Please refer to Course Team for Tutorial times, course TAs, and their office hours.

Tutorial Exercises

Tutorials will be based on the lecture from the previous week, not the current week.

Solutions for Tutorials will be posted the Monday following the week of the tutorial exercise.

All tutorial notes have been prepared by Kevin Zhang.
